I will maintain a tradition of excellence by pushing the boundaries of creativity, blending art with technology, while adhering to the highest standards of quality and professionalism. I aim to consistently evolve with advancements in design and animation technologies so that my work remains relevant in an ever-changing digital landscape. What is needed to become a Graphic Designer?

Graphic designers are individuals responsible for create visual concepts communicate a message. To become a graphic designer, you need a combination of skills, qualifications and experience.

Skills

The skills to be a graphic designer are as follows, but not limited to:

    Technical Skills

    • Typography skills
    • Design Principles
    • UX and UI Design
    • Colour Theory
    • Branding
    • Digital Technology (HTML, CSS, JS)

    Soft Skills

    • Creativity
    • Time Management
    • Collaboration Skills
    • Communication Skills
    • Detail-Oriented
